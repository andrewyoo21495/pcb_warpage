Elevation Encoder (Stochastic)

CNN layers Conv32→64→128→256
spatial pool AdaptiveAvgPool2d(4×4)
flat dim 256 × 4 × 4 = 4096
MLP hidden 512
activation LeakyReLU(0.2)
logvar clamp [-4.0, 4.0]
output z₁ dim = 64

block 3 64→32 · 32→64

final ↑2× + Conv(16→1)
